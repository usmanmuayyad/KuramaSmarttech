Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and delivery times?
We offer nationwide shipping across Nigeria with delivery times of 2-5 business days for major cities and 3-7 business days for other locations. Express shipping options are available for urgent orders.
Do you offer warranty on your products?
Yes, all our products come with manufacturer's warranty ranging from 6 months to 2 years depending on the product. We also provide additional store warranty for extra peace of mind.
Can I return a product if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 7-day return policy for unused products in original packaging. For defective products, we provide immediate replacement or repair under our warranty terms.
Do you support bulk orders for businesses?
Absolutely! We offer special pricing and customized solutions for bulk orders, corporate gifts, and business partnerships. Contact our sales team for volume discounts.
